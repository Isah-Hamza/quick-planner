Features

Real Time Reporting

Submit reports on unsafe acts and conditions as they happen.

Customizable Forms

Tailor report forms to match your organization’s specific needs.

Push Notifications

Receive alerts for new reports, updates, and required actions.

Data Analytics

Analyze trends and generate insights from submitted reports.

AI Scanning

Utilize AI to scan photos for unsafe acts and generate reports.

Multi platform support

The app is available on Web, Android (version 6 and above) and iOS (version 13 and above).

Offline Data Caching

Report incidents even without internet access; data syncs when connection is restored.

Location Tagging

Automatically tag the location of reported incidents.

Data Exports Options

Export reports in various formats(Excel, PDF or CSV) for further analysis. With the help of data analytic tools, trends and insights are generated from submitted reports.

Safescout provides an easy-to-use platform for reporting and managing workplace safety incidents.
